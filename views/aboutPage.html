What is FTSE Ventures?

FTSE Ventures predicts which way companies listed on the FTSE 100 & 250 will move


How?

The site looks at four primary factors to calculate a final score out of ten

1. Moving Average – Calculate the 50 & 200 day moving average

2. Analyst Predication – Look at how banks and analysts predict the price will move

3. Director Dealing – Find which Directors are actively trading
